Scheduled maintenance!

The intervals for scheduled overhauls are determined by the vehicle manufacturer, where they are usually every 10 or 15 thousand km.

We carry out this review applying original parts and products and with the correct specifications in accordance with the instructions of the owner's manual. Thus, when you choose to carry out your scheduled overhaul at the Bosch Car Service Network, you will have the same quality of services and parts guaranteed by a dealership, but with a lower value.
